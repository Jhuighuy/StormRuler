Detailed Description

template<legacy_vector_like Vector>
class Storm::GmresSolver< Vector >

The GMRES (Generalized Minimal Residual) linear operator equation solver.

GMRES is typically more robust than the BiCG type solvers, but it may be slower than the BiCG solvers for the well-conditioned moderate sized problems.

GMRES is algebraically equivalent to MINRES method in the self-adjoint operator unpreconditioned case, however, the need for restarts may lead to the much slower GMRES convergence rate.

GMRES may be applied to the singular problems, and the square least squares problems, although, similarly to MINRES, convergeance to minimum norm solution is not guaranteed.
